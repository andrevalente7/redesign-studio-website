FAQs

Are you a traditional marketing agency?

No. ReDesign Studio works as your outsourced Marketing Team, combining strategy, execution and AI-powered systems into one ongoing partnership.

Can we pause or cancel?

Yes. You can pause or cancel when your business priorities change.

What can we request?

You can request work across growth strategy, AI systems, landing pages, content, performance marketing, ad creatives, funnel optimization, email marketing and social media management within the monthly capacity.

What is not included?

Specialist services, custom development, business operations support, unlimited deliverables, 24/7 communication, daily meetings and large-scale enterprise projects are outside the monthly subscription.

What happens in the first month?

We onboard your business, complete the audit, set up access, review competitors, define KPIs, build the roadmap and launch the first priority initiative.

Who is this best for?

It is best for founder-led and growing businesses that have a proven offer and want consistent marketing execution without hiring a full in-house team.

What happens after I book a call?

We review your goals, identify growth gaps and recommend the next best steps. If the Marketing Team subscription is a fit, we explain how onboarding would work.

Do I need everything ready before starting?

No. If your goals, assets or systems are messy, we organize the starting point during onboarding.

Do you replace our internal team or support them?

Both are possible. We can operate as your outsourced Marketing Team or support your internal team with strategy, execution capacity and AI-powered systems.
